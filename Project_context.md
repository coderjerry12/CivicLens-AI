# CivicLens AI

## Project Vision

CivicLens AI is an AI-powered hyperlocal community issue reporting and resolution platform that enables citizens to report public infrastructure issues while helping local authorities efficiently manage, prioritize, and resolve them using Artificial Intelligence.

The platform aims to improve transparency, accountability, and citizen participation through intelligent automation, geolocation, image analysis, and real-time tracking.

The application should feel like a modern SaaS product rather than a hackathon prototype.

---

# Problem Statement

Communities often struggle to report public issues such as potholes, water leakages, garbage accumulation, damaged streetlights, and road hazards.

Current reporting systems are fragmented, lack transparency, and rarely provide meaningful tracking.

CivicLens AI solves this problem by allowing citizens to report issues with AI assistance while enabling authorities to efficiently manage resolutions.
